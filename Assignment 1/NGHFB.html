Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds



Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds is an English rock band that formed in 2010 after the breakup of Oasis. Oasis was a rock band that gained popularity in the 90's. Oasis's most notable members were brother Noel and Liam Gallagher. A fight between the brothers caused Noel to quit the band and form NGHFB.

NGHFB's most recent album, "Who Built the Moon?", was realeased at the end of 2017. The album ventured more into psychedelic rock and pop rock. While the album is not my favorite of Noel Gallagher's, I enjoyed the album and saw NGHFB on his North American release tour.
